The Halfpipe Suite

Bold, spacious, and designed for those who love a little adrenaline in their aesthetic, The Halfpipe Suite brings the smooth curves of skate architecture indoors.

The room features custom-built, halfpipe-inspired wall elements and shelving, creating a flowing, sculptural backdrop that feels both modern and playful. Soft neutral tones contrast with industrial touches—brushed metal, concrete textures, and deck-art accents—giving the space an elevated skate-lodge vibe. A cozy lounge nook offers the perfect spot to unwind after a long day of riding, while premium bedding and upgraded amenities make the stay effortlessly comfortable. The Halfpipe Suite blends style and skate culture into a uniquely immersive experience you won’t find anywhere else.
